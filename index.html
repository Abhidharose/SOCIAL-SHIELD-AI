SocialShield Architecture

SPECIFICATION V1.4

A deep dive into the multi-agent neural layout of SocialShield AI.

Our Mission

The modern web has evolved from simple phishing emails to sophisticated, conversational social engineering campaigns. Attackers use generative AI models to construct deepfakes, custom urgency copy, and realistic brand replicas.

SocialShield AI leverages a **Collaborative Agent Mesh Network** architecture. Instead of processing input through a singular model, we break threats down into distinct vectors, analyzing each using specialized localized models before establishing a consensus score. This ensures low latency, highly explained results, and bulletproof security.

Multi-Model Consensus Scoring

When a threat audit runs, the individual outputs of PsyAgent (nlp), LinkAgent (domain/SSL), DeepfakeAgent (media scan), and MetaAgent (headers) are weighted based on the query type. If LinkAgent detects an immediate domain flag, its weight spikes, shifting the consensus rating to high-risk regardless of safe linguistics.
